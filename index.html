Sonia Sotomayor

An American lawyer and jurist

Here's a time line of Sonia:

  • June 25 1954 - Born in Bronx New York
  • 1963 - Sonia is Diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes
  • 1976 - Sonia Graduates from Princeton University
  • JUN 1979 - Graduates from Yale Law School
  • AUG 1979 - Joins Manhattan District Attorneys Office
  • 1980 - Sonia Sotomayor joins the Board of Directors of the Puerto Rican Legal Defense and Education Fund.
  • 1984 - Joines Law Firm Pavia & Harcourt
  • 1988 - Sonia serves on the New York Campaign Finance Board
  • 1992 - Begins Tenure as a Federal District Court Judge
  • 1997 - President Bill Clinton NOminates Sonia Sotomayor to the US Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit.
  • 1998 - Sonia Confirmed to the US Court of Appeals
  • 2001 - Delivers "A Latina Judge's Voice Speech"
  • Jul 2009 - Begins US Supreme Court Hearings
  • Aug 2009 - Sonia is confirmed by the US Senate, Becoming the First Hispanic Supreme Court Justice
  • Sep 2009 - US Supreme Court Hears Challenge to Election Funding Restrictions on Corporations
